## Testing Plan

### Week 1: Launch & Learn ($175)
- Launch Campaign 1 (3 ad sets, 2 creatives each) + Campaign 3 (warm-up)
- Don't touch anything for 3 days minimum
- Monitor: CTR, CPC, CPM, link clicks

### Week 2: Optimize ($250)
- Kill ad sets with CTR < 1% or no conversions after $25 spend
- Launch Campaign 2 (retargeting) with site visitor audience
- Shift warm-up budget to winning ad sets

### Weeks 3-4: Scale ($575)
- 80% budget on top 2-3 performers
- 20% on retargeting
- Test 1-2 new creatives against winners
- Target: $5-15 CPA on starter, $15-25 CPA on meadow
